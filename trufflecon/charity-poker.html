Ante up for social good at TruffleCon’s first-ever charity poker event!

NOVEMBER 6th & 7th | 6:15pm PST

About the Event

Get ready to go all-in for charity at TruffleCon 2020! This year, we are partnering with The Giving Block to host a poker event choc-full of well-known blockchain superstars to raise funds for non-profit organizations.

All ticket proceeds will go directly to qualified 501(c)3 charities hand-picked by the Truffle Team. And, the players that bluff their way to the final table will receive a sweet piece of the total prize pool.

Virtual poker tables open immediately after last session on Friday, November 6th at 6:15pm PST. Friday's games will be timed and fast-paced to allow players to play at 6-8 tables. The top money players from day 1 will be narrowed down to form the final table for day 2 starting after the last session on Saturday, November 7th at 6:15 PST .

Charity Prize Pool

Donation pools will be comprised of player entry fees and be dispersed by the top 4 players to the Giving Block charity of their choice:

  • 1st place - 40% to charity of choice
  • 2nd place - 30% to charity of choice
  • 3rd place - 20% to charity of choice
  • 4th place - 10% to charity of choice

Player Prize Pool

Although this event is for charity, the top 10% of players will win prizes as well! The prize pool is comprised of sponsor donations and will be paid out in progressive amounts. Final amounts and prize items/swag are still being gathered and will be announced on the first day of the tournament.
