THE SWEET FEATURES AT THE CORE OF GANACHE

  • THE ETHEREUM BLOCKCHAIN ON YOUR DESKTOP

    GANACHE ships with an internal Javascript implementation of the Ethereum Blockchain which has additional programmatic capabilities - no local clients need to be installed!

  • VISUAL MNEMONIC & ACCOUNT INFO

    Quickly see the current status of the generated accounts, including their addresses, private keys, nonces and balances.

  • BLOCKCHAIN LOG OUTPUT

    See the output of web3 calls to the blockchain, along with responses and other vital debugging info.

  • INSTANT MINING CONTROLS

    Stop, start, force mine a single block, and take or revet multiple snapshots of the blockchain as and when you chose.

  • BUILT IN BLOCK EXPLORER

    Examine specific blocks or transactions at will and see detailed information about the parameters.

  • BLOCKCHAIN CONNECTED REPL

    Complete access to a Node.js powered REPL that you can execute commands in, have full access to an initialize web3 object, and many useful commands to make development testing easier.
